How We Met

Claire and Jonny’s first date was at a Mexican restaurant near their workplace. Claire thought Jonny was very thoughtful by choosing a nearby place and Jonny was excited they had a happy hour!

Jonny warned Claire that he would be dressed like a palm tree, showing up in a Hawaiian shirt and with a sweat towel.

Claire wore her first date outfit that is tasteful enough for work, but with the change of a button works perfectly for a date!

Originally Claire had said she would have to leave to pick up a friend from the bus station, a lie Jonny saw through immediately and poked fun at. During the date one of Jonny’s friends “happened to” stop by, but Claire didn’t mind as he left quickly and giving his stamp of approval; Jonny didn’t need an out.

As the date continued, Claire’s friend magically didn’t need to be picked up anymore!

A few days after their first date, Claire had to travel for work and much to her liking, Jonny was texting and calling her every day.

Everything was going swimmingly when all of a sudden Covid... To be safe, Jonny asked Claire to his parent’s house in Maine. Thinking it would just be a weekend (a week at most) Claire brought five pairs of underwear and no sweatpants. Jonny’s folks, Lisa and Mitch, didn’t mind and welcomed her with open arms.

The spikes of covid were stressful, but Claire found a home and safe place. Not just in Maine but in Jonny.

Eventually, Claire drove back home to be close to her parents, Jonny soon wanted to make the visit. He came to Minnesota, got to see Mark Larson’s hometown of St. Paul (inside joke) and loved introducing the whole Larson family to new food. From there, the families were hooked and so were Jonny and Claire!

The culmination of their relationship happened in a vineyard in Argentina, where Jonny proposed to the love of his life, Claire.

They haven’t looked back and are so excited to celebrate the future together!
